Architecture Decision: Signed Event Ledger, NOT Blockchain

Corda evaluated and rejected. Known parties under AU law don't need Byzantine fault tolerance. ASX CHESS failure (A$250M write-off) makes DLT a credibility risk. We get 95% of auditability at 10% of complexity: Ed25519 signatures, Merkle tree tamper evidence, optional hash anchoring to Ethereum. PostgreSQL at $50/mo vs Corda cluster at $500+/mo.
